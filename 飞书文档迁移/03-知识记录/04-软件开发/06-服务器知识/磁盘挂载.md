磁盘挂载
服务器重启，磁盘重新挂载
sudo fdisk -l 
sudo mount /dev/vdb /iflytek/
新建磁盘
临时挂载
fdisk -l
格式化（格式化前查看是否已经被挂载过，是否磁盘掉挂载了）
mkfs -t ext4 /dev/vdb    =常用
mkfs -t xfs /dev/vdb 
挂载
mkdir /iflytek
mount /dev/vdb /iflytek
mount /dev/vdb /iflytek-share
https://blog.csdn.net/sinat_32724581/article/details/127404188
远程挂载
showmount -e 172.30.95.158
mount -t nfs 172.30.95.158:/iflytek-share/minio /miniodata
mount -t nfs -o nolock 172.30.95.158:/iflytek-share/minio /miniodata
